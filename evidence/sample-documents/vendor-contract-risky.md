Confidential Vendor Operations Agreement
Document ID: TEST-RISKY-VENDOR-2026
Classification: Strictly Confidential
Effective Date: June 10, 2026
Expiration Date: June 10, 2027

1. Parties

Party A: NorthBridge Health Analytics Ltd.
Party B: Atlas Managed Infrastructure Services Ltd.

Party B will provide on-premises maintenance, monitoring, and emergency support for Party A's private data processing environment. Party B staff may access customer records, internal operating metrics, financial worksheets, privileged system accounts, and architecture diagrams.

2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes customer PII, employee records, operating dashboards, internal financial data, credentials, network diagrams, incident notes, scripts, and any information marked confidential by Party A.

3. Service Response Time Requirements

P1 (Critical) Data center outage, data breach risk <=15 minutes <=4 hours
P2 (High) System performance degradation affecting >30% users <=30 minutes <=8 hours
P3 (Medium) Minor system issues, no user impact <=2 hours <=24 hours
P4 (Low) Documentation updates, routine maintenance <=24 hours <=7 business days

4. Data Processing Requirements

Data Localization: All Party A's data must be processed and stored exclusively on Party A's on-premises servers. No data may be transferred to any cloud service or off-site location.

Encryption: All data in transit must use TLS 1.3 encryption; all data at rest must use AES-256 encryption.

Access Control: Party B must implement least privilege access. Staff may only access systems necessary for assigned work.

Data Retention: Party B may not retain any copies of Party A's data after service completion.

Audit Logs: Party B must maintain complete access logs for all data operations for a minimum of 180 days.

5. Personnel and Subcontractors

All Party B personnel must pass background checks before access is granted. Party B must provide Party A with a monthly list of authorized personnel. Party B may not subcontract services without prior written approval from Party A.

6. Confidentiality

Party B shall not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without Party A's written consent. Party B shall return or securely destroy all Confidential Information upon termination. Confidentiality obligations survive the termination of this agreement for a period of 3 years.

7. Liability

In the event of a breach of confidentiality by Party B, Party B shall pay liquidated damages equal to 20% of the total contract value. Party B remains responsible for direct and indirect losses caused by its breach.

8. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising from this agreement shall first be resolved through negotiation. If negotiation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the Shanghai Arbitration Commission.

9. Miscellaneous

This agreement is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
