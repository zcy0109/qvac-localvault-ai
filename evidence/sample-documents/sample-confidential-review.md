# Confidential Vendor Review Sample

Acme Research Services will provide document classification and internal knowledge-base support to the customer. The agreement states that all customer files must remain confidential and may not be shared with third-party cloud AI providers without written approval.

The vendor promises an initial response time of two business days for ordinary support requests. Critical incidents should receive a first response within four hours. The agreement does not specify an uptime service level, backup schedule, or breach notification period.

The customer may terminate the agreement for material breach after giving the vendor ten days to cure. Payment is due within thirty days of invoice receipt. The agreement is silent on whether late payments accrue interest.

The vendor may use subcontractors for infrastructure maintenance, but the document does not list approved subcontractors or require flow-down confidentiality obligations.

Open questions:

- Is local-only processing required for every document class?
- Does the vendor maintain audit logs for document access?
- What happens if confidential information is accidentally processed by an external service?
- Are subcontractors required to follow the same security obligations?
