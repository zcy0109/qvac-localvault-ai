Confidential Vendor Support Agreement
Document ID: TEST-PARTIAL-VENDOR-2026
Classification: Strictly Confidential
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
Expiration Date: July 1, 2027

1. Parties

Party A: Meridian FinTech Systems Co., Ltd.
Party B: HarborCloud Operations Ltd.

Party B will provide private infrastructure support services for Party A's internal payment analytics environment. The services may require access to customer PII, financial records, production dashboards, security configurations, and operational runbooks.

2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes customer data, financial reports, credentials, diagrams, source snippets, security configurations, and any written or oral information marked confidential by Party A.

3. Service Response Time Requirements

P1 (Critical) Data center outage, data breach risk <=15 minutes <=4 hours
P2 (High) System performance degradation affecting >30% users <=30 minutes <=8 hours
P3 (Medium) Minor system issues, no user impact <=2 hours <=24 hours
P4 (Low) Documentation updates, routine maintenance <=24 hours <=7 business days

4. Data Processing Requirements

Data Localization: All Party A's data must be processed and stored exclusively on Party A's on-premises servers. No data may be transferred to any cloud service or off-site location.

Encryption: All data in transit must use TLS 1.3 encryption; all data at rest must use AES-256 encryption.

Access Control: Party B must apply least privilege access and remove access for departing staff within one business day.

Data Retention: Party B may not retain any copies of Party A's data after service completion.

Audit Logs: Party B must maintain complete access logs for all data operations for a minimum of 180 days.

5. Incident Notification

Party B shall notify Party A in writing within 24 hours after becoming aware of any actual or suspected data breach or security incident involving Confidential Information. The notice must include known impact, affected systems, containment steps, and the responsible incident owner.

6. Audit Rights

Party A has the right to audit Party B's data processing activities, security controls, access logs, personnel lists, and subcontractor records upon reasonable notice. Party B must cooperate with audit evidence requests and remediate confirmed findings within an agreed remediation period.

7. Confidentiality

Party B shall not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without Party A's written consent. Party B shall return or destroy Confidential Information upon termination. Confidentiality obligations survive the termination of this agreement for a period of 3 years.

8. Liability

In the event of a breach of confidentiality by Party B, Party B shall pay liquidated damages equal to 20% of the total contract value.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute shall be submitted to the Shanghai Arbitration Commission if negotiation fails.

10. Miscellaneous

This agreement is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China. Amendments must be signed by both parties.
